Pollution

Pollution is the introduction of contaminants into the natural environment that cause adverse change.

Major forms of pollution include:

  1. Air pollution,
  2. light pollution,
  3. littering,
  4. noise pollution,
  5. plastic pollution,
  6. soil contamination,
  7. radioactive contamination,
  8. thermal pollution,
  9. visual pollution,
  10. water pollution.
